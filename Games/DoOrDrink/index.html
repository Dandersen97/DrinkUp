    How To Play

    Each turn a player will be given a task to complete. They can either do this task and receive full points or drink their way out of it for half points. First to 21 points wins. Player order is randomized each round so there's no advantage, but some luck is involved.

    There are multiple packages of tasks to select, each with it's own theme. Add/remove packages based on player comfortibilty and/or props available.
